Leean (Yuet Ki) Ning is a PhD student in the Faculty of Education at the University of Hong Kong. Prior to her academic career, she worked as an entrepreneur for an education consulting firm, an early childhood educator for both private and public schools across continents, and an admissions officer for an online high school program. Her current research focuses on teacher interactions with generative AI in K-12 education and its impact on student motivation and engagement.
